Laser Engraving

Laser engraving uses a laser beam to mark or burn a design onto a surface. It can be used on wood, glass, acrylic, leather, and metal. I like this topic because it mixes creativity with technology.

A simple item can become personal when someone adds a name, logo, picture, date, or special message to it. This is why laser engraving is popular for gifts, decorations, awards, and business products.

Laser engraving also takes patience and planning. The design has to be lined up correctly, and the settings have to match the material being used.

Common Materials

  • Acrylic
  • Leather
  • Glass
  • Metal tumblers
