What Is Laser Engraving?

Laser engraving is a way to burn or mark a design onto materials like wood, acrylic, glass, leather, and metal. It can be used for gifts, business signs, decorations, and custom projects.

Image source: Pixabay or another copyright free image site.

Popular Things to Engrave

Many people like laser engraving because it makes regular items feel personal and custom. A simple design can turn into something meaningful.

  • Wood signs
  • Custom cups
  • Keychains
  • Photo frames
  • Business logos

Why I Like Laser Engraving

I like laser engraving because it mixes creativity with technology. You can start with a basic idea on a computer and turn it into a real item that someone can keep or use.
